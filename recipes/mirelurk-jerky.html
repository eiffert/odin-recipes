What on God's Earth is it?

A shishkebab, made from mirelurk meat.
Increases Perception and Poison Resistance.



Ingredients:
  • Anti-freeze bottle x1
  • Mirelurk meat x2
  • Tarberry x2

Method:
  1. Cut the mirelurk meat into bite-sized chunks.
  2. Place the mirelurk chunks and tarberries inside the anti-freeze bottle.
  3. Shake well to squash the berries and marinate the chunks.
  4. Hold the anti-freeze bottle over a heat source to cook the meat.
  5. Caution: Be careful not to accidentally melt the bottle or you will end up with acid and plastic instead!
  6. Remove meat once cooked, and skewer like a shishkebab.

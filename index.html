The real Hell's Kitchen!

Despite living in a nuclear wasteland, there are a surprising number of easy and delicious recipes for you to discover. You will find cooking pots, campfires, pre-War stoves, and rotisserie spits (if you know where to look!) out there in the world.

They all facilitate the cooking of various foods, drinks, and aid items from ingredients found in the wastes, or grown in settlements!

Forget your inner Ramsay because this stuff's far from fine dining, but beggars can't be choosers!

What on God's Earth is it?

Roasted slices of ant meat prepared at a cooking station. It restores 45 Hit Points upon consumption.


Ingredients:
  • Ant meat x3
    •

    Method:
    1. Cut the ant meat into slices approximately 5cm thick.
    2. Using metal mesh or chain link fence over a fire source, grill the meat on either side.
    3. Once the meat has distinct grill markings, it's ready!
